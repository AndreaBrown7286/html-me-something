Why I go camping.

I consider camping to be one of the best activites a family can take part in. Some of my earliest and fondest memories are of being at a campgrouds, playing at the playground or the lake with new friends, sitting around a campfire roasting marshmellows with family before going to bed in my grandparents camper. I am happy to say I fell in love with camping as a child and I am proud to carry on a tradition by teaching my children the same love.

What to do while camping.

I could go on all day telling you what activites there are to do while camping, so I will simply list my favorite things to do:

  • fishing
  • hiking
  • bike riding
  • swimming or playing in a creek/river
  • laying in a hamack reading a book
  • playing games with friends
  • cooking over a campfire

Where I have gone camping.

I honestly can't remember the name of everywhere we have been but I will list afew of my favorites.

  • Randolph County Lake, Il
  • Lake Murphysboro, Il
  • Washington County Lake, Il
  • Carlyle, Il
  • Johnson Shut-ins State Park, Mo
  • Garrison River Resort Steelville, Mo

The places I would like to go.

The list of places I would like to visit and camp is very long, most national parks in the US including Yellow Stone, Alaska State park and Niagra Falls state park, to name a few.

My favorite adventure yet.

My favorite camping trip is also an anual camping trip I take with freinds and family to Johnson Shut-ins every summer. We swimm in the rivers and creeks, have huge cookouts, and hick at near by Elephant Rock state park. It is beutiful and always an adventure.
